Maulana Bisyir Azhari

I am a PhD Candidate in Electrical Engineering at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), where I work at the Unmanned System Research Group (USRG) under the supervision of Prof. David Hyunchul Shim. My research focuses on Robotics, Autonomous Drones, Localization, and Computer Vision — particularly visual odometry, SLAM, and autonomous navigation systems.
