**Held by:** Republic of Korea Agency for Defense Development (ADD)<br>
**Duration:** October 2022<br>
**Location:** Daejeon, South Korea

## Overview

This competition, organized by Korea's Agency for Defense Development (ADD), challenged teams to develop a **fully autonomous drone system for reconnaissance in a battlefield indoor environment**.

The drone had to:
- Enter and navigate an unknown, GPS-denied indoor environment
- Perform autonomous localization and obstacle avoidance
- Explore the environment autonomously
- Detect and identify specified targets
- Complete all tasks using **onboard computation only** (no external computing)

## Technical Approach

- **Autonomous Exploration Algorithm** – Frontier-based exploration to systematically cover unknown indoor spaces
- **Object Detection-Localization** – Euclidean clustering for real-time 3D target localization
- **LiDAR SLAM** – Onboard LiDAR-based Simultaneous Localization and Mapping for precise indoor navigation
