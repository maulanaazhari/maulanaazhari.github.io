I am a PhD Candidate in Electrical Engineering at [Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST)](https://www.kaist.ac.kr/), where I work at the [Unmanned System Research Group (USRG)](https://unmanned.kaist.ac.kr/) under the supervision of [Prof. David Hyunchul Shim](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=-CKm5DEAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao). My research focuses on **Robotics**, **Autonomous Drones**, **Localization**, and **Computer Vision** — particularly visual odometry, SLAM, and autonomous navigation systems.
